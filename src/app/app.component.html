Tips for Family Travel in Egypt

Family travel in Egypt can be challenging. The country can be messy, both politically and figuratively. Planning a trip to Egypt requires some work. here are a lot of areas of Egypt where it's either not advisable, or not possible to travel without a guide. Like many middle-east countries, the main language in Egypt is Arabic. Most Egyptians speak English as a second language, but it's always a good idea to know some important Arabic words and phrases to help break the ice.

Slumber on the Nile

It's the longest river in the world and is the central focus of many myths and legends, which is why spending your time on the Nile River is a must.

Best Time To Visit Egypt

December and January are considered peak season so you'll find long lines at iconic sites, Resort prices also soar during these months.

Visit Abu Simbel

It's located at the very southern part of Egypt and is accessible by a short flight from Aswan but this out-of-the-way tomb is so impressive.

The Valley Kings

The Valley of the Kings is a must see day trip in Egypt, to explore all Egyptian tombs of Seti, Rameses, Tutankhamun and other Egyptian pharaohs.
